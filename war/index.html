Both from small towns, we moved to the city to create our own lives. We met on the way out to a metal club, joined by his best friend and my sister. It turned out that the company was just too good not to find out where this might go.

There was a connection that was unmistakable, that something special we where both looking for. Over the next couple of years we became best friends, sharing our lives, hopes and dreams.

Then one sunset afternoon, on a cruise ship over a big lake, came that magical moment when he went down on one knee to ask "the BIG question". And the rest is not as they say history, but the beginning of a wonderful new life for us.

"Meeting you was fate, becoming your friend was a choice, but falling in love with you was beyond my control" ..Anonymous

We have found the person that does not just say I love you, but the person that means it every time. The person that we want to go to bed with every night and wake up next to every morning. This is our beginning, our start to something wonderful.
